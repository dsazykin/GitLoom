## 4 · The unified timeline (gates before dates)

The four source calendars, reconciled. Each stage is entered by its gate, not its date; "an act slips before an asset lies" (LaunchCampaignPlan) governs everything downstream of L1.

| When / gate | Fundraising (F) | Marketing (G) | Sales (J) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Now → client GA** (Jul–Sep 2026) | WBSO S&O filing (before-the-month rule); advisor visit **August 2026** (brief + deck ready); VFF plan aligned to GTM phases | Pre-launch build-in-public cadence; waitlist nurture A1–A5; essays publish as ready | 40-candidate list built; ~25 discovery interviews; design partners recruited (no selling) |
| **L1 — Act One: client launch** (gate: client GA-ready) | Traction slide starts filling (no empty-slot presentations) | Show HN act one + first-hour FAQ; launch-week posts; press kit variant 1 | Paid media Phase 1 (≤ €1.5K); interviews continue |
| **L1 → L2 interlude** | Board-update cadence begins (report-back template) | One substantial piece / 2 weeks; release notes per Voice Bible Appendix D | 3–5 Track B pilot teams qualified (6-month, time-boxed) |
| **L2 — Act Two: control center** (gate: P2-12 demonstrably works against two real bot-PR sources) | Seed conversations on act-two evidence; swarm-vision deck usable | Manifesto at L2 − 3–5 days; Show HN act two; wedge campaign; Phase 2 paid burst (€5–9K) | Paid pilots open; compliance persona still nurture-only |
| **2027+** (gates: governance tier ships; beta unit economics match Cloud Companion §5) | MIT feasibility proposal (April 2027 round); Innovatiebox after first WBSO year | Scale phase (€8–15K/mo) if funnel metrics hold | Team-tier selling opens (ends the "do not sell to Priya" rule); NL → DACH |
